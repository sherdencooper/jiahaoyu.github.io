Interests

Inspired by Zhiyuan Honor Program which encourages talented undergraduate students to do research, I joined NSEC Lab in my first year at school. From then on, I set secure machine learning as my research interest. Specifically, I have done some research about Backdoor attack, Membership Inference attack and Model inversion attack. From my third year, I started to do some theoretical work about DP, privacy and robustness.
